Colaboremos

A collaborative selling platform for women small business owners

What was it about?

- As COVID-19 struck the world and there was widespread lockdown, small business owners were affected badly.
- In order to support women small vendors, we started working on a platform to connect them buyers.
- This project was aimed at creating a non-profit platform for product discovery from nearby small vendors.


As a Product Manager

- Ideated and refined the idea by creating user persona, customer journey maps.
- Prioritized sections of the application to deliver a Minimum Viable Product.
- Created detailed features and stories based to convert idea into a product through incremental steps.
